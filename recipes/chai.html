Masala Chai

Description

Masala chai is an indian beverage made by brewing black tea with fragrant spices, sugar and milk.

Ingredients

Masala Chai Powder

  • 2-3 cloves
  • 1/2 cinnamon stick
  • 4 green cardamoms
  • 2 black pepper corn

Masala Chai Tea

  • 1/2 inch piece of peeled and chopped ginger
  • 3 tsp loose leaf black tea
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 3-4 tsp sugar
  • 1 cup full fat milk

Steps

  1. Crush spices finely in grinder
  2. Heat water in sauce pot and add powdered spices, ginger, and black tea
  3. Bring to a rolling boil on medium and boil for 2-3 minutes
  4. Next, add sugar and pour milk and bring again to a rolling boil and simmer 2 mins or until tea is dark.
  5. Aerate masala chai by using a ladle and scooping tea out and in
  6. Lastly, strain your tea to serving cups and drink!
